# TAM Accounts (Total Addressable Market)

**Purpose**: Identify, discover, and qualify target accounts that match your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP).

## Use Cases

- **ICP-Based Account Discovery**: Search for companies matching specific criteria
- **Account Fit Scoring**: Multi-criteria evaluation (firmographics + technographics)
- **Vertical Market Identification**: Find accounts in specific industries/niches
- **TAM Expansion**: Discover new segments and markets

## Company Size Applications

- **SMB**: Focus on fast ICP discovery with lower enrichment costs
- **Mid-Market**: Balance between volume and depth of account intelligence
- **Enterprise**: Deep account profiling with multiple stakeholder analysis

## Examples in This Folder

- **icp_account_finder_agent.py** (Tier 3) - Autonomous agent searches and scores accounts by ICP fit
- **multi_agent_fit_scoring.md** (Shelf) - 5 specialized AI agents each check specific ICP criteria
- **vertical_market_identifier/** (Toolkit) - Identify and analyze niche vertical opportunities
