# Segmentation & Targeting

**Purpose**: Score, qualify, and segment leads/accounts to optimize sales focus and outbound strategy.

## Use Cases

- **Lead Scoring**: Assign numerical scores based on fit + engagement signals
- **Intent Detection**: Identify buying intent from behavioral signals
- **Account Segmentation**: Group accounts by characteristics for targeted campaigns
- **Territory Assignment**: Route accounts to the right sales rep/team

## Company Size Applications

- **SMB**: Simple scoring rules (3-5 criteria), fast qualification
- **Mid-Market**: Multi-dimensional scoring (10+ signals), predictive models
- **Enterprise**: Autonomous learning systems that improve over time

- **autonomous_lead_scoring_agent.py** (Tier 6) - Self-improving scoring system
- **intent_detection_workflow.json** (Tier 2) - Detect buyer intent from signals
