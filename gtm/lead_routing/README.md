# Lead Routing

**Purpose**: Intelligently route leads to the right person, team, or workflow based on characteristics and signals.

## Use Cases

- **Email Triage**: Classify incoming emails by intent and route accordingly
- **Lead Assignment**: Route leads to sales reps by territory, segment, or availability
- **MQL Routing**: Send marketing qualified leads to appropriate SDR/AE
- **Account-Based Routing**: Route contacts at strategic accounts to AEs

## Company Size Applications

- **SMB**: Simple rules-based routing (geography, deal size)
- **Mid-Market**: AI-powered intent detection + round-robin assignment
- **Enterprise**: Complex routing with account ownership, team capacity, skillset matching
