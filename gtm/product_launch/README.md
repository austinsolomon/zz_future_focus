# Product Launch & Campaign Orchestration

**Purpose**: Coordinate complex, multi-channel product launches and marketing campaigns.

## Use Cases

- **Product Launch Planning**: Coordinate cross-functional launch activities
- **Campaign Orchestration**: Multi-channel campaign execution
- **GTM Strategy Execution**: Turn strategy into automated workflows
- **Launch Retrospectives**: Analyze launch performance

## Company Size Applications

- **SMB**: Simple launch checklist automation
- **Mid-Market**: Multi-channel coordinated campaigns
- **Enterprise**: Complex launches with 10+ teams, recursive planning

- **product_launch_orchestrator.py** (Tier 5) - Claude Code orchestrates complex launches
- **campaign_launch_toy.py** (Tier 5 Toy) - Teaching example for recursive orchestration
