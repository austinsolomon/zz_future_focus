# Automate Prospecting with Clay (SheetWiz / Tech-Proxy)

## Objective

Target a niche persona (Finance pros in Tech) by checking DNS records to see if a company uses Google Mail (proxy for Google Sheets) and referencing past Excel-heavy roles.

## Tool Stack

- **Clay** - Workflow Orchestration
- **WhoisXML API** - DNS/MX Record Lookup
- **LinkedIn Data** - Role History
- **AI** - Duration Calculation

## Workflow Summary

This automation uses technical signals to identify ideal prospects by:
1. Checking company DNS/MX records via WhoisXML to detect Google Workspace usage
2. Analyzing LinkedIn history for finance professionals
3. Identifying past roles that involved heavy Excel/spreadsheet work
4. Calculating role duration and experience levels with AI
5. Building a list of finance pros at Google-using companies who understand spreadsheet pain points
