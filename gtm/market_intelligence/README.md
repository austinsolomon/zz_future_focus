# Market Intelligence

**Purpose**: Monitor market trends, signals, and emerging opportunities.

## Use Cases

- **Startup Signal Detection**: Track funding rounds, product launches
- **Market Trend Analysis**: Identify emerging trends in your space
- **TAM Expansion**: Discover new market segments
- **Timing Signals**: Identify when companies are in buying mode

## Company Size Applications

- **SMB**: Basic trend monitoring (1-2 key trends)
- **Mid-Market**: Automated signal detection across multiple sources
- **Enterprise**: Comprehensive market intelligence platform
