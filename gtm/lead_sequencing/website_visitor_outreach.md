# Automate Website Visitor Outreach (Inbound-Led Outbound)

## Objective

Capture anonymous website visitors and convert them into outbound leads by enriching their data and drafting a personalized email that a human must approve in Slack before sending.

## Tool Stack

- **RB2B** - Visitor ID
- **Clay** - Enrichment & AI Drafting
- **Slack** - Human Approval Interface
- **Smartlead** - Email Sending

## Workflow Summary

This automation bridges the gap between anonymous web traffic and qualified outbound leads by:
1. Identifying visitors through RB2B
2. Enriching contact data via Clay
3. Generating personalized email drafts with AI
4. Requiring human approval through Slack before any outreach is sent
5. Executing approved emails through Smartlead
