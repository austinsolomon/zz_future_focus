# Legal Technology Toolkit

## Special Task: Building a Responsive Legal Framework for Tech Threats
Creating a more flexible and responsive legal framework to address technological threats, combining AI ethics expertise, legal knowledge, and AI engineering with Claude Code.

## Mission
Build a faster, more adaptive legal system that can keep pace with rapidly evolving technology threats through intelligent automation and AI-assisted legal research and drafting.

## Quick Start

Each automation directory contains:
- `README.md` - Detailed documentation
- `automation.yaml` - Automation configuration
- Implementation files (Python scripts, Claude prompts)
- Legal templates and examples

## Integration Points

- **Legal Databases**: Westlaw, LexisNexis, PACER
- **Document Management**: iManage, NetDocuments
- **Case Management**: Clio, MyCase
- **Research Tools**: Fastcase, vLex
- **Claude Code**: AI-powered analysis and drafting

## Core Principles

1. **Speed Without Sacrificing Rigor** - Automation accelerates routine work
2. **Adaptive Frameworks** - Laws that evolve with technology
3. **AI Ethics Integration** - Build ethics into legal structures
4. **Precedent Intelligence** - Learn from tech-related case history
5. **Proactive Compliance** - Prevent issues before they arise

## Tier Definitions

- **Tier 1**: Simple, deterministic automations (citations, templates)
- **Tier 2**: Context-aware with AI (contract review, research summaries)
- **Tier 3**: Autonomous agents with complex decision-making (regulatory analysis, framework validation)

## Philosophy

Technology law moves faster than traditional legal processes. These automations help:
- **Accelerate** legal research and drafting
- **Analyze** complex regulatory landscapes
- **Anticipate** emerging threats and compliance needs
- **Adapt** legal frameworks to technological change
- **Advocate** for responsive, ethical AI governance
